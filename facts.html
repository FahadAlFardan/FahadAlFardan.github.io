Facts About Elephants


  • Elephants are the worlds largest land animal!


  • Elephants can live up to 70 years in the wild and reach maturity at the age of 15.


  • There are only two species of elephant, Asian and African Elephants.


  • They have 150,000 muscles in their trunks.


  • These gentle giants can eat up to 150 kilograms of food every single day.


  • Sadly, 90% of African elephants have been wiped out due to poaching or habitat loss in the last 100 years.


  • Elephant tusks are actually teeth


  • Elephant calves can stand and walk within 20 minutes of being born
