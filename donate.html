How to contribute

People all over the world can help protect elephants and save future generations from extinction by contributing via donating to organizations which look after the well being and best interests of these beautiful giants.There are different types of organizations which have different purposes such as organizations that look after orphaned elephants which have lost their mothers or herds due to poaching. Others are sanctuaries for elephants rescued from abusive captivity. Lastly, anti poaching organizations need as much help as they can get in order to protect wild elephants from poachers. These heroes work around the clock in the fields of game reserves to crack down on poachers and make a difference by saving one elephant at a time.It is our duty as the human race to work together and ensure the survivial of elephants before they are all gone.
